Ingredients

  • 2 eggs.

  • 1/2 cup of milk or milk substitute

  • 1 serving of kodiak cakes pancake mix.

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl.

  2. Mix well, add more milk for thinner pancakes.

  3. Pour into preheated skillet and cook over medium heat.

  4. Flip when you start to see bubbles popping in batter!

  5. Top with your favorite syrup and fruits.
